Reorder List

Aug 3, 2016 | Leetcode | Hits

This is the solution about Leetcode-Reorder List.

Description:

Given a singly linked list L: L0→L1→…→Ln-1→Ln,
reorder it to: L0→Ln→L1→Ln-1→L2→Ln-2→…

You must do this in-place without altering the nodes’ values.

For example,
Given {1,2,3,4}, reorder it to {1,4,2,3}.

Solution:

Analysis:

First solution:

  1. Iterate the list and store all nodes into ArrayList
  2. insert the node into original list until the middle of list
  3. make the next pointer of the middle to null.
  4. Time complexity and space complexity is O(n)

Second solution:

  1. At first find out prior of the middle
  2. reverse the half after middle
  3. reorder the list from the head and the middle.
  4. Time complexity is O(n)and space complexity is O(1)
